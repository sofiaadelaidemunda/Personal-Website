My Favorite TV Shows by Category

K-Drama

When Life Gives You Tangerines (2025)

Director: Kim Won-seok
Writer: Lim Sang-choon
Stars: IU, Park Bo-gum, Moon So-ri

Set in Jeju, a spirited girl and a steadfast boy’s island story blossoms into a lifelong tale of setbacks and triumphs, proving love endures across time.

True Beauty (2020–2021)

Director: Kim Sang-hyeop
Stars: Moon Ga-young, Cha Eun-woo, Hwang In-youp

A high school girl masters makeup to hide her insecurities, but learns to embrace her true self while navigating love and friendship.

W – Two Worlds Apart (2016)

Director: Jung Dae-yoon
Stars: Lee Jong-suk, Han Hyo-joo

A surgeon is pulled into the world of a webtoon, where she meets its hero and faces a dangerous crossover between fiction and reality.

Love in the Moonlight (2016)

Director: Kim Seong-yoon
Stars: Park Bo-gum, Kim Yoo-jung

A Crown Prince falls in love with a young woman disguised as a eunuch, leading to a tender romance in the Joseon dynasty.

Action / Anime

Attack on Titan (2013–2023)

Directors: Tetsurō Araki, Masashi Koizuka, Yuichiro Hayashi
Stars: Yuki Kaji, Yui Ishikawa, Marina Inoue

In a world besieged by man-eating Titans, humanity fights for survival behind massive walls, uncovering dark secrets along the way.

Jujutsu Kaisen (2020–)

Director: Sunghoo Park (Season 1), Shōta Goshozono (Season 2)
Stars: Junya Enoki, Yuma Uchida, Asami Seto

Yuji Itadori, a high schooler who swallows a cursed object, joins the Tokyo Jujutsu High to battle deadly curses and protect humanity.
