Paris, France

"Paris is the city of love" as they say. This is one of my dream destinations specifically for its Eiffel Tower. I would love to view the city lights at night and admire the infrastructures built that are like ancient.

Aside from the Eiffel Tower, I’m excited about exploring world‑class museums like the Louvre and Musée d'Orsay, strolling along the Seine, and people‑watching in cafés with a croissant and espresso. The historic neighborhoods of Montmartre and Le Marais look so charming—with cobblestone streets, street artists, and hidden gardens. French fashion and pastries draw me in too; I’d love to window‑shop on the Champs‑Élysées and sample macarons from Ladurée. I also dream of visiting Notre‑Dame and the Sainte‑Chapelle’s stained glass on a sunny afternoon.
