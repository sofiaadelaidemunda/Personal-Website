Switzerland

Switzerland became one of my dream places to visit because of how beautiful the country is through photos I saw. I feel like it is so peaceful to live there, it just screams that kind of vibe. I also love how the place looks like it's a twist of modern and at the same time old-fashioned style.

The Swiss Alps call to me with their snow‑topped peaks, and I fantasize about taking the Glacier Express train through mountain tunnels and lakes. I’d love to try real Swiss chocolate and cheese fondue in a cozy chalet, and to take boat rides on Lake Geneva or Lake Lucerne. Everything looks so spotless and orderly; I can imagine cycling through picturesque villages like Zermatt with the Matterhorn looming overhead. Switzerland’s efficient public transport and multilingual culture (German, French, Italian) make it feel both adventurous and comfortable for a traveler.
